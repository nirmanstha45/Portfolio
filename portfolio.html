Black Diamond Salon

Local Business Growth & Lead Generation

The Problem

Low customer bookings despite an active social media presence. Their organic content was getting likes, but it wasn't translating into appointments and revenue.

The Strategy

Shifted focus from brand awareness to targeted local conversion campaigns using strong, offer-based creatives tailored to their geographical radius.

Execution

  • Meta ads optimization
  • Retargeting engaged users
  • A/B creative testing

The Results

3X ROAS
  • Significantly increased bookings
  • Drastically lower cost-per-lead

Renovation Services Pvt. Ltd.

High-Ticket B2B Lead Gen

The Problem

They were relying on cold outreach with a high acquisition cost and low close rates for commercial renovation contracts.

The Strategy

Implemented a dual-channel strategy (Google Search Ads for intent-based leads + Meta for retargeting authority).

Execution

  • Google Ads intent targeting
  • High-converting landing pages
  • CRM integration for tracking

The Results

4X ROAS
  • Consistent qualified lead flow
  • Scaled revenue predictability
